Frequently Asked Questions

Below are answers to the most common questions about our Resume Builder Platform.

  • How do I create a resume?

    You can create a resume by choosing a template, filling in your personal information, and exporting the resume.

  • Is there a cost for using the platform?

    Our basic features are free, but premium templates and features come with a subscription.

  • Can I edit my resume after saving it?

    Yes, you can access your saved resumes anytime and make edits as needed.
